A Business Proposal

About the K-Drama

A Business Proposal is a 12‑episode romantic comedy about Shin Ha‑ri, a food researcher who goes on a blind date to help her friend — only to discover her date is her CEO, Kang Tae‑moo. Pretending for six weeks while hiding her identity, she navigates office antics, family drama, and fake engagements that unexpectedly spark real feelings.

Main Characters

Kang Tae‑moo (Ahn Hyo‑seop): The Harvard‑educated CEO with a stone‑face exterior that hides warmth and comedic charm.
Shin Ha‑ri (Kim Se‑jeong): An energetic, determined food researcher — her comedic timing and honesty make her irresistible.
Jin Young‑seo (Seol In‑ah): The pampered heiress whose bond with Ha‑ri shows the power of sisterhood.
Cha Sung‑hoon (Kim Min‑kyu): Tae‑moo’s loyal secretary who finds his own sweet romance.
